Colwyn Bay

❮ ❯
×

During a trip to Colwyn Bay via university to view photography exhibitions, I took the opportunity to photograph parts of this beachside town as it was my first time in the area. I have ideas for future projects in this town, focusing on the erosion of high streets and these seemingly becoming derelict coastal towns. These images however focus on a prettier side, showcasing the iconic pier and objects from the beach.
