Isometric Practice

This series of isometric design was created during my time at college. While only a select few can be seen here, I treated this assignment as an exercise and created an abundance of isometric patterns simply for the sake of practice. Many of these designs are incredibly visually appealing to me, and although I have no applications for them as of now, they are art pieces in themselves.
