Yard Life

Yard Life, or known by the book title "My Life With Horses", is a brief photobook created by myself featuring objects, animals and scenery that depict the daily life of working with a horse. I created this project as part of a university module, and although it was made for that purpose, this is a project to be expanded on. This project utilises both digital and film photography in 120 format.
